(Q4) What is a switch statement, and when is it preferred over if-else?

SWITCH statement

switch executes the code blocks that matches an expression. switch is often used as a more readable alternative to many if...else if...else statements, especially when dealing with multiple possible values.

The switch statement is often preferred over if-else when evaluating a single expression against numerous potential constant values.
